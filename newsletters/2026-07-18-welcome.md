# A Word From The Chapel

Beloved friends of the Shepherd's Chapel,

Welcome to the very first issue of our weekly letter. Each week we shall share
a word of encouragement, news of the congregation, and the times of our
gatherings.

## This Week At The Chapel

- Sunday service at half past nine in the morning
- Wednesday evening prayer at seven
- The choir practises on Thursday, and new voices are always welcome

## A Thought To Carry

The lamp in the chapel window is lit every evening, as it has been for many
years. It is a small thing, but it says to anyone passing in the dark that
someone is expecting them.

Come as you are. There is a seat for you.

Yours in fellowship,
**Reverend Marius**
